Starc, Head, Ravindra among 1,166 players in IPL 2024 Auction Register

Some of the biggest names have registered for the auction of the Indian Premier League (IPL), but Jofra Archer, who was recently released by the Mumbai Indians, has not enrolled. A total of 1,166 players find themselves in the Register, shared with the IPL franchises on Friday. The auction is scheduled to take place in Dubai on December 19.

The Road to the West Indies and USA 2024

A historic, 20 teams will form part of the event and will be divided into four groups of five. Twelve teams have already secured their places for the 2024 show piece. As hosts, the West Indies and the USA take the first two spots, then, the top eight teams in the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup Australia 2022 (top four in each Super 12 group) gained their places, along with Afghanistan and Bangladesh who were the next best teams on the MRF Tyres ICC T20 International Rankings cut-off date of 14 November 2022.
